VIDHAR App: Account & Data Deletion

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with control over your personal data. This page outlines the process for requesting the deletion of your VIDHAR account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account and data, please follow this simple step:

  1. Send an email to our support team from the email address associated with your VIDHAR account. Clicking the button below will open your default email client with a pre-filled template.

Request Account Deletion via Email

Our team will process your request and confirm the deletion via email within 7-10 business days.

What Data is Deleted?

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following personal data associated with your account:

  • User Profile: Your name, email address, roll number, and any other profile information.
  • Class Information: Records of classes you have created (as a teacher) or joined (as a student).
  • Attendance Records: All historical data of your attendance, including dates, times, and location data used for verification.
  • Device ID: The unique device identifier linked to your account.

Data Retention

Once deleted, your personal data cannot be recovered. We do not retain any personal user data after the deletion process is complete. Anonymized, non-personal data (e.g., app crash logs that contain no user information) may be retained for analytical purposes.
